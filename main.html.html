About Lasbela

Lasbela District is located in southeastern Balochistan along the Arabian Sea. The district headquarters is Uthal. It connects Balochistan with Karachi and plays an important role in trade, industry, and coastal tourism.

History

Lasbela became a district in 1954 and has historical roots linked with coastal trade routes. The region has a rich cultural and tribal heritage.
